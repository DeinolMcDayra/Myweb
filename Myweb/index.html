About Me

Hi, I'm Deinol, a second-year student studying Informatics Engineering at Muhammadiyah Surakarta University. I’m passionate about exploring new ideas and expanding my knowledge, especially in the tech field. My fascination with understanding how things work is what led me to start programming. I also enjoy problem-solving, as it allows me to approach challenges from different perspectives and develop innovative solutions.

With the rapid advancement of technology, the rise of artificial intelligence has only deepened my curiosity. I often find myself captivated by the intricate processes behind AI systems—the algorithms, data structures, and mathematical models that power them. The complexity behind AI is what makes it so fascinating; the way it transforms raw data into intelligent predictions and decisions is nothing short of remarkable. This blend of logic and creativity fuels my enthusiasm to learn more about how these systems function and how they can be leveraged to solve real-world problems.

Currently, I’m delving deeper into web development, focusing on back-end technologies, and have been experimenting with Next.js. Additionally, I’m diving into machine learning and data science, areas that truly capture my interest. Understanding AI’s inner workings, from neural networks to data preprocessing, has been an exciting journey, and I’m eager to keep pushing my boundaries in this field.
